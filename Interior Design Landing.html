About Us

Soirée & Tre enables access to a venue for hosting events but this is more than a venue; it's land we've cherished and is family owned and managed. It is truly one-of-a-kind scenery for a real, natural backdrop.

The owner of the property has a strong commitment to protect the beauty and natural integrity of the location while still enabling and successfully hosting memorable events—from small gatherings and camp experiences to major launch festivals like the Meghalaya Age, NH7 Weekender—marking a memorable achievement for small family owned businesses.

We are looking to partner with the right people and businesses who share our vision for quality and respect for this special space. Since we run things directly, you get consistent, personal attention from the team. Let's build the amazing event you're dreaming of - get in touch with us.
